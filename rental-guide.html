Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent in Metro Vancouver?

+
Expect $1,800–$2,400/mo for a 1-bedroom condo, $2,200–$3,000+ for 2-bedroom. Surrey and Fraser Valley are most affordable at $1,400–$1,800/mo.

Is it free to use a realtor to find a rental?

+
In most BC rental transactions, the landlord or property manager pays the agent's fee. As a tenant, you typically pay no direct cost.

What does BC's Residential Tenancy Act protect?

+
BC's RTA protects tenants from unlawful rent increases, provides security of tenure, governs eviction rules, and sets standards for repairs and maintenance.

How competitive is the Vancouver rental market?

+
Vancouver's vacancy rate is below 1% — one of the lowest in Canada. Acting quickly and having a strong application (credit check, employment letter, references) is essential.
