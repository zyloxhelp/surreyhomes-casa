About Silverdale

What is Silverdale, Mission Like?

Emerging master-planned community above Mission with mountain and valley views. One of Fraser Valley's largest planned developments with thousands of homes. This makes Silverdale one of Mission's most distinctive communities, attracting residents who value its particular combination of lifestyle, convenience, and community character.

Silverdale is served by West Coast Express commuter rail, connecting residents to the broader Metro Vancouver network. The neighbourhood's location within Mission provides access to Mission School District 75, parks, and community amenities.

The real estate market in Silverdale features primarily new homes and pre-sales, appealing to a mix of buyers seeking this specific lifestyle. Whether you're relocating to Mission or moving within the area, Silverdale deserves serious consideration.
