Complete BC Home Buyer's Guide 2025

Everything you need to know about buying a home in Metro Vancouver and Fraser Valley BC — from pre-approval to getting the keys.

Get Free Buyer Consultation ›
1

Get Pre-Approved

Before searching, get a mortgage pre-approval. It confirms your budget, locks your rate for 90-120 days, and shows sellers you're serious.

2

Hire a Realtor

As your buyer's agent, I provide MLS access, neighbourhood expertise, and negotiation skills — at no direct cost to you as the buyer.

3

Search & Tour

I'll set up custom MLS alerts and personally tour properties with you, flagging potential issues before you fall in love.

4

Make an Offer

When you find the right home, I'll prepare a competitive offer with appropriate subject conditions to protect you.

5

Subject Removal

During 7-14 days, complete inspection, finalize financing, review strata docs. Then remove subjects — the deal is firm.

6

Get the Keys!

On completion day, funds transfer, title registers, and you get the keys. Welcome home!
