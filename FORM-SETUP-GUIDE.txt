Forms will send leads to TWO places:
1. GHL CRM → Contacts (auto-created)
2. Netlify Forms → Dashboard + Email notification

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
 NETLIFY EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS SETUP (5 minutes)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

After deploying the new ZIP:

1. Go to: app.netlify.com → Your Site → Forms
   → You'll see: consultation-form, modal-consultation, rental-inquiry

2. Click on any form → "Form notifications"
   → Add notification → Email notification
   → Email: info@abhirairealtor.com
   → Save

3. Repeat for all 3 forms

Now every submission → Email to info@abhirairealtor.com instantly!

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
 GHL CRM — LEADS APPEAR HERE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

GHL Dashboard → Contacts → filter by tag "website-lead"

Each lead will have:
- Name, Email, Phone
- Service interest (Buy/Sell/Rent/Invest)
- Message
- Page they submitted from
- Tag: "website-lead"
