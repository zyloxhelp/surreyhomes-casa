Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to sell in Metro Vancouver?

+
Spring (March–May) and early fall (September–October) are traditionally the strongest. However, properly priced homes sell well year-round. In 2026, spring is shaping up as particularly strong.

How do you determine the right listing price?

+
I conduct a Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) looking at recent sales of similar properties nearby, current active listings, and market trends. The goal is to attract maximum buyers while achieving your best outcome.

How long will it take to sell my home?

+
Well-priced homes in Metro Vancouver typically sell in 10–21 days. My average is 14 days. Strategic pricing from day one is the single most important factor.

What can I do to maximize my sale price?

+
Declutter and deep clean, address deferred maintenance, consider professional staging, and invest in quality photography. I'll give you a specific prep checklist during our free evaluation.
