FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Property Transfer Tax (PTT) in BC?

+
BC's PTT is 1% on the first $200,000, 2% on $200,001–$2M, and 3% above $2M. First-time buyers may qualify for a full exemption on homes under $500,000, saving up to $7,700.

Do I need a pre-approval before searching for homes in BC?

+
Yes — a mortgage pre-approval is essential before starting your home search. It tells you your exact budget, strengthens your offer, and prevents disappointment. Most BC lenders offer pre-approvals valid for 90–120 days at no cost.

How long does it take to buy a house in BC?

+
Typically 2–4 months from starting your search to getting the keys. Pre-approval: 1–5 days. Search: 2–6 weeks. Subject removal: 7–14 days. Completion: usually 30–60 days after accepted offer.

What are strata fees and what do they cover?

+
Strata fees are monthly payments for living in a condo or townhouse in BC. They cover building maintenance, common areas, building insurance, and contingency reserve fund. Expect $250–$600/month for condos in Metro Vancouver.
