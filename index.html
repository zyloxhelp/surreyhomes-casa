FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Property Transfer Tax (PTT) in BC?

+
BC's PTT is 1% on the first $200,000, 2% on $200,001–$2M, and 3% above $2M. First-time buyers may qualify for a full exemption on homes under $500,000, saving up to $7,700.

Do I need a pre-approval before searching for homes in BC?

+
Yes — a mortgage pre-approval confirms your budget, strengthens your offer, and prevents disappointment. Most BC lenders offer free pre-approvals valid for 90–120 days.

How long does it take to buy a house in BC?

+
Typically 2–4 months. Pre-approval: 1–5 days. Search: 2–6 weeks. Subject removal: 7–14 days. Completion: usually 30–60 days after accepted offer.

Is the buyer's agent free in BC?

+
In most BC transactions, the buyer's agent commission is paid by the seller. As a buyer, you typically pay no direct realtor fee — I represent your interests at no cost to you.
